Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail

Powertrain and Performance

  1. Engine:
    • The Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail is powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine.
  2. Power Output:
    • Produces approximately 593 horsepower (600 PS) and 840 Nm of torque, ensuring effortless performance.
  3. Driving Dynamics:
    • Maintains the signature "magic carpet ride" with supreme comfort and dynamic handling.
  4. Transmission:
    • 8-speed automatic gearbox delivers smooth, silent shifts for an unparalleled driving experience.

Bespoke Coachbuilt Design

  1. One-of-a-Kind Creation:
    • Designed for a specific client, making it one of only four Droptail models ever created.
  2. Design Inspiration:
    • Inspired by the Black Baccara rose, a dark and velvety rose beloved by the commissioning family.
  3. Exterior Aesthetic:
    • Two-Tone Paint Finish:
      • Combines rich red hues and black tones to mimic the Black Baccara rose petals.
    • Complex Paint Process:
      • Multi-layer paint process achieves a dynamic color shift under light.
    • Parthenon Grille:
      • Hand-polished stainless steel grille for timeless elegance.
    • Aerodynamic Sculpting:
      • Smooth, flowing lines and retractable roof technology.

Interior

  1. Artistic Craftsmanship:
    • Handcrafted with over 1,600 individual wood veneer pieces arranged to form rose petal patterns.
  2. Materials:
    • Premium materials such as open-pore wood, leather, and metal accents.
    • Dark Santos Palisander wood enhances the organic theme.
  3. Color Theme:
    • Deep red tones reflect the Black Baccara rose.
  4. Removable Hard Top:
    • Transforms into a roadster with its detachable carbon-fiber hard top.
    • Features a starlight headliner with rose-inspired patterns.
