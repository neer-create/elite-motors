Mercedes AMG ONE

Performance
1. F1-Derived Hybrid Powertrain
  • Engine: 1.6L turbocharged V6 hybrid (directly derived from Mercedes-AMG's Formula 1 engine).
  • Electric Motors:
    • One on each front wheel.
    • One assisting the crankshaft.
    • One integrated into the turbocharger.
  • Total Power Output: Over 1,049 hp.
2. Hybrid Technology
  • Combines gasoline and electric power for efficiency and performance.
  • Recuperation system harnesses energy during braking and coasting, improving battery charge.
3. Performance Stats
  • 0–62 mph (0–100 km/h): 2.9 seconds.
  • 0–124 mph (0–200 km/h): 7.0 seconds.
  • Top Speed: 219 mph (352 km/h).
  • All-wheel drive with fully variable torque distribution.
Interior
1. Driver-Focused Cockpit
  • F1-inspired steering wheel with integrated controls.
  • 10-inch high-resolution screens: One for instrumentation, one for infotainment.
2. Minimalist Design
  • Lightweight bucket seats with fixed backrests.
  • Alcantara and carbon-fiber finishes.
3. Tech Features
  • Limited infotainment for a driver-centric experience.
  • Advanced telemetry system for performance data tracking.
